Kit_1.0 HomeLab Requirements.

Powerful & Low Cost!!! The above parts list is my best take at assembling a homelab for around 2k. The goal for this list is to make a micro data center at good price point.

Pros: Low cost, Low power consumption, Powerful processing power

Cons: Loud when running at full power, Rack only scales to 2u, Heavy
