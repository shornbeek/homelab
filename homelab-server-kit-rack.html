Build The Rack!

In this guide we are putting together the iStarUSA 4u rack.

iStarUSA 4-post open frame rack 4u unboxing

In this video we are unboxing the iStarUSA 4-post open frame rack 4U from B&H Photo. I have pre built half of the frame offline.

iStarUSA 4-post open frame rack. Assembly of 1/2 of the 4u rack

In this video we are putting together the other half of the 4u server rack. I rebuild wat was built offline. Use M6 mat black screws and nuts to hold all your server components together.

iStarUSA 4-post open frame rack. Assembly of the 4 crossbars

In this video we are connecting the left and right sides that were built in the previous videos. We are using the remaining 4 bars and m6 screws and nuts to complete the main construction of the server rack.

iStarUSA 4-post open frame rack small 4u bar installation

In this video we are adding the small 4u bars and looking at the casters. The 4u marks face out towards the back of the unit. If you are installing the casters I would assemble on the top then flip the whole unit over. This video ends the series on how to build the iStarUSA 4u open frame server rack. BE SURE TO TIGHTEN ALL YOUR SCREWS before moving on.
