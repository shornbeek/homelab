Install the rack servers power supply boxes!

In this guide we are installing the HP proliant power supplies.

Step 1. Removing the Hp Proliant DL360p g8 Cover to view internal processers.

In this video we are removing the top of the HP proliant cover to access the power supply boxes. You can slide the power supply into the back of the HP proliant server without taking off the top. I wanted to view the processer and show how to add in the power supply into the server with the top cover of the rack server removed. We will also quickly review the Hp proliant g8 server before the 2 power supplies are installed.

Step 2. Comparing the HP Proliant dl380 g7 vs g8 review

In this video we take a quick look at the older HP Proliant dl380 g7 layout compaired to the new HP Proliant dl380 g8 layout. View the differnces between the two HP proliant 1u rack servers. (Skip this video if you want to install jump to installing the power supply into the rack server)

Step 3. Installing HP Proliant Dl360p g8 DL360 Teardown or Opening. Install the Power supply.

In this video we are adding in the power supply to the server. Slide in the boxes under the rack crossbar. The power supply boxes for the rack server have to be perfectly horizontal when sliding into place. You don't need to remove the server to install the power supply. press the power supplies into place. The Hp Proliant server has a clip that will click in once the power supply is completely installed.

Step 4. Plugging in HP Proliant DL360p g8 1 foot power cable to the CyberPower supply

In this video we are plugging in the server's power cables into our CyberPower power supply. We are giving your server power by attaching the HP Proliant dl360 power supply boxes to the CyberPower Suppressor. I have replaced the power supply cable that came with the Hp Proliant server with a 1 foot power cable. Now your server will have power once the Main rack encloser power supply is plugged in.
