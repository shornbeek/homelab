Why buy a Homelab when I could use the Cloud?

Everyone says that you should host your data on the could, that it cost less and is easy to use. For most people this is true.

Most people will never see or own an enterprise server. The majority of users needs can all be solved by hosting data in the cloud.

But... for those of you that want a home server... I understand and Welcome!

Lets take a look at the different tyes of servers

Your options are to buy a tower server, a rack server, or a super powerful blade server. Take a look at the pros and cons of each below.

  • Tower Server
    • $500 - 4000
    • Pros
    • Scalability and ease of upgrade
    • Cost-effective
    • Cools easily
    • Con
    • Consumes a lot of space
    • Provides a basic level of performance
    • Complicated cable management
  • Rack Server
    • $100 - 20,000
    • Pros
    • Failure containment
    • Simplified cable management
    • Cost-effective
    • Con
    • Power usage
    • Maintenance
    • Noisy
  • Blade Server
    • $5,000 - 68,000
    • Pros
    • Load balancing and failover
    • Centralized management
    • Size and form-factor
    • Con
    • Expensive configuration
    • HVAC
    • Noisy

    Rack Servers have millions of differnent models and configurations.
